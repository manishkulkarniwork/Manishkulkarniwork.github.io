About

I'm Manish Kulkarni, a Multimedia Producer and Content Strategist based in New York City, part of the founding team at Evolve Pictures LLC. I handle projects end-to-end: from concept and planning through production, post-production, and delivery.

I've produced 40+ live-streamed events for audiences of 500+, directed four narrative and documentary films, and created a 68-Reel Instagram series that generated 3.50M views in 68 days. I hold a Master's in Media Studies and Management and bring 4+ years of hands-on experience across live events, films, and social content.

What I find most rewarding about production is the execution — coordinating crews, operating multi-camera setups, and managing the logistics that turn an idea into something people actually see and respond to. I show up, solve problems, and take ownership of the result.
